STEMlights

STEMlights is a bi-weekly newsletter that aims to introduce students in a global community to relevant STEM News. As a Celebrate program, STEMlights ensures the bridge between reality and STEM, to promote an appealing learning environment for all students. Our newsletter highlights different STEM opportunities and news, while shining light on intersections where STEM can be applied, developed, and learned. Read the latest edition of STEMlights here.

As a competitive newsletter, by teens for teens, our goal is to express STEM sentiments in a creative manner. All members within STEMlights ensure to provide unbiased, clear-cut news that aims to encourage and implement equitable educational opportunities. Our newsletter covers STEM Breakthroughs, Student Opportunities, Youth Leaders, Politics, and a Coronavirus Awareness section. With our wide coverage of STEM topics, our team promotes growth and excellence through education.

With coordination from each team member, our newsletter currently serves nearly 500 subscribers, providing them with content such as unbiased COVID-19 news and remote opportunities. Recently redesigned, STEMlights furnishes the growth of knowledge from student-to-student. Carefully curated content permits our student-run newsletter to be both competitive and excellent in quality. Our team has worked hard to release over 10 newsletters that are currently opened amongst numerous high schools and colleges accross the U.S.

Our program, being student-run, offers to benefit both students and the community. Students are able to gain a deeper understanding of the STEM world, being that it forever-changes.
