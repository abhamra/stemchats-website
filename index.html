STEMchats is a student-led nonprofit on a mission to increase the accessibility of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education for all students, regardless of their racial, gender, or socioeconomic backgrounds.

We believe in educational equity in the STEM fields: all students deserve a quality STEM education. Our programs, ranging from workshop series to research initiatives, are all completely built by students, for students.
