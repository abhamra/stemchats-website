üöÄ Our Mission

STEMchats is a student-led nonprofit on a mission to shatter barriers in science, technology, engineering, & mathematics (STEM) for underrepresented students through our peer-to-peer teaching philosophy, aiming to ultimately inspire these same students to create even further impact in the future.

We believe in educational equity in the STEM fields: all students deserve a quality STEM education, regardless of their racial, gender, or socioeconomic background. Our programs, ranging from workshop series to research initiatives, are all completely built by students, for students.

üîç Our Differences

As a completely student-led nonprprofit organization, we understand how other students want to learn. This allow us to make use of the peer-to-peer technique which leads to the creation of engaging STEM outreach programs & initiatives that are students for other students.

Moreover, our team consists mostly of first-generation, low-income, minority, LGBTQ+, and/or female students. Being educated at mostly underfunded schools and identifying as underrepresented in the fields of STEM, we understand the struggles of trying to find STEM immersion and education opportunities at a young age. Most importantly, the backgrounds of our team members helps us relate to the students we aim to impact.
