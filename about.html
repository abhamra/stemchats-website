About Us

As a student-led nonprofit, STEMchats aims to democratize STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) education for all by bringing impactful STEM programming as a way to provide equal opportunities for disadvantaged students. We are guided by our four core values: inclusive, collabrative, encouraging, and laidback.

We emphasize these values through both our workplace culture as well as our programs, which are geared towards empowering disadvantaged students to use the power of STEM for social good.
