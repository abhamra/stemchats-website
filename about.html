Our Story

Many STEM outreach organizations exist, including both student-led and adult-led; however, STEMchats is unlike any of them. Our student-led organization allow us to capitalize off of youth insights and enterprise to drive bottom-up social change in order to offer two unique business propositions: diversity and exponential impact.

DIVERSITY. Our team is mostly first-generation, low-income students (FGLI), as well as women and LGBTQ+ students. This puts us in a unique position to fully understand the problems that these disadvantaged communities face and allows us to build groundbreaking educational initiatives to tackle the root of the problem.

EXPONENTIAL IMPACT. Our funneling model builds long-lasting relationships between STEMchats members and our target communities. Through our selective fellowship program, the most promising students impacted by our initiatives join us as full team members and eventually create their own STEM outreach programs.
