Workplace Culture

We are Inclusive.

STEMchats thrives on its diversity of backgrounds, experiences, and ideas! This means that we have a zero-tolerance policy on discrimination, hate, or bullying. All of our team members are expected to be inclusive of others on the team and make everyone feel valued regardless of race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, location, sex, gender, or any other factors.

We are Collaborative.

STEMchats relies on teamwork and collaboration between all members of the organization to uphold our founding principles: celebrate, stimulate, innovate. We encourage appropriate collaboration, both within each team and across both programming and business-development departments.

All team members are expected to actively participate in assigned collaborations or work to come up with new ways to work with members outside their direct team. The Leadership Team does their best to facilitate this kind of interaction through transparency, connection, Slack, and in-person meetups.

We are Encouraging.

STEMchats succeeds thanks to positive energy and reinforcement from everyone on our team! We often hype each other up on social media, on calls, and in our dedicated #random Slack channel. Every team member is encouraged to contribute to discussions with the assurance that their opinions will always be valued. We feel that this is the best way to build up both individual and collective confidence, helping us break out of our shells!

STEMchats works on uplifting underrepresented students while creating a close and supportive network in the process. Our STEMchats team members who are in college are always down to answer any questions about the admissions process. Since many of us are first-generation, low-income (FGLI) college students, we understand the confusion. We are all here to support and encourage one another across personal and professional spheres

We are Laidback.

STEMchats is chill. No, really! Just like most startups and modern GenZ organizations, we love sharing memes over Slack and keeping a light, laidback mood during video calls. We have non-work related channels that every team member can join: #college, #opportunities, #memes, & more.

This doesn’t mean we don’t get our work done. In fact, we’ve found that sometimes getting off-topic ultimately increases our productivity, making any long calls or action items more manageable through building team comradery! We are all friends and hope that everyone on the team can build strong bonds beyond work and geographic location.
